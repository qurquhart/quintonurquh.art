Voice Over

For this project I fostered a comfortable recording environment for my client in which we captured high quality audio recordings for a demo. The takes were processed with the iZotope RX plugin suite and a multitude of instruments and plugins were used to create custom backing tracks for varying voice-over techniques.

Post Sound

In this example I coordinated the transfer of .OMF files from my client's project, processed said audio files, and genereated deliverables for the final cut. To do so I de-noised, compressed, mixed, and matched room tone across all takes.
